# Mottainai Pantry v1.0.3 release notes

## Overview

This release candidate prepares Mottainai Pantry for public release. It improves the information shown during closed testing and keeps the Japanese, English, and Simplified Chinese experiences aligned.

## Highlights

- Refined guidance for AI recipe suggestions, saved recipes, and local recipe ideas
- Aligned Japanese, English, and Simplified Chinese labels and terminology
- Updated screenshots for Home, AI Recipe Suggestions, Import from Photos, and Statistics
- Improved guidance for backup and restore, barcode lookup, and statistics
- Updated the website language switcher, SEO metadata, and support information

## Notes

The app is currently in Google Play Closed Test. AI and food-related information is for reference; users must make their own food-safety and allergy decisions.
