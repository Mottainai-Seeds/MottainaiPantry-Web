Frequently asked questions

Answers about Mottainai Pantry, AI features, local data, and planned services.

What do I need for AI features?

You need your own OpenAI or Google Gemini API key. The key is encrypted on your device with Android Keystore, and provider usage fees are your responsibility.

Is on-device AI available?

Not yet. During closed testing, ChatGPT, Gemini, and a mock provider are available. On-device AI without an API key is planned for a future update.

Where is my data stored?

Inventory, recipes, and settings are stored locally by default. Cloud sync is not currently provided.

Are Supporter and Premium available?

They are planned services. Billing is not implemented during closed testing, so they cannot currently be purchased.

Is there an iPhone version?

Mottainai Pantry is currently Android only. An iOS version may be considered in the future.
