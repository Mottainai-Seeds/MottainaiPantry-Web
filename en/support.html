Support for Mottainai Pantry

Contact us about questions, problems, or feedback related to version 1.1.0.

Contact

Email mottainaiseeds@gmail.com. Never include API keys, passwords, or other sensitive personal information.

Reporting a problem

Please include your device model, Android version, app version, the screen where the problem occurred, steps to reproduce it, and a screenshot when possible.

Feedback

Feature suggestions are welcome and may be used to guide future development.

Latest updates

Development updates are also posted on the official X account.

Mottainai Supporter

Mottainai Supporter is a one-time Google Play purchase that supports development. It is separate from Mottainai Premium, which is not currently available. Local AI is also still in development.
