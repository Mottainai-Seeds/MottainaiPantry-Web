Join the Android closed test.

Mottainai Pantry is currently available through Google Play closed testing.

How to participate

Closed testing requires an invitation and a Google account registered for the test. Open the participation link while signed in with the invited account.

Before installing

This is a test build. Features and stored-data formats may change, so keep a backup of important data.

Availability

The iOS version and public Google Play release are not available yet. Updates are announced on the official X account.

v1.2.1 Closed Test update

AI recipe suggestions are more reliable and can consider more of your registered food. Photo import, history restore, and deadline actions are also easier to use.

Mottainai Supporter is a one-time Google Play purchase for supporting development. Mottainai Premium and Local AI are not currently available.
