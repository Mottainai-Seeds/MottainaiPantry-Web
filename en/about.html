About Mottainai Seeds

A small independent project building practical tools for a less wasteful everyday life.

Our purpose

We want to turn overlooked food, time, ideas, and habits into small seeds that improve daily life and the future.

Our approach

We value simple language, trustworthy behavior, warm design, and features that work in real households.

Mottainai Pantry

Our first app focuses on household food inventory and helps people notice, use, and enjoy ingredients before they go to waste.

Current status

Mottainai Pantry v1.1.0 is a Release Candidate for Google Play closed testing. It stabilizes the Home dashboard, quick food entry, statistics-to-Home filtering, and backup and restore verification. Mottainai Premium and Local AI are not currently available.
