Privacy Policy

How Mottainai Pantry handles personal information and app data.

Local data

Food inventory, recipes, saved recipes, and settings are stored locally on your device by default. The developer does not collect or store this data.

AI services

When you use an AI feature, ingredient names and other request content may be sent to the selected provider, such as OpenAI or Google Gemini, using the API key you enter.

API keys

API keys are encrypted on your device using Android Keystore. Provider terms, privacy policies, and usage charges apply.

Exports and backups

You are responsible for managing CSV exports, imports, backups, and restored files securely.

Contact

For privacy questions, email mottainaiseeds@gmail.com.
